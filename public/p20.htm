Whispers of Autumn

Whispers of autumn softly sigh,
Through the rustling leaves they fly.
Golden hues paint the sky,
As summer bids a sweet goodbye.

In the breeze, a gentle chill,
Nature's breath, serene and still.
Crimson trees on distant hill,
Echoing a tranquil thrill.

Leaves cascade, a vibrant dance,
Nature's artistry, a fleeting trance.
In this moment, we advance,
Captivated by nature's romance.

of autumn, tender and true,
A symphony of gold and blue.
In this season, we renew,
In the whispers of autumn, we find our cue.
