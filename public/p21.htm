Eternal Echoes

In the quiet depths of night's embrace,
Where stars weave dreams across the space,
Whispers linger, soft and low,
Echoes of the past aglow.

Through misty realms where memories dwell,
In shadows cast by ancient spell,
Voices murmur, faint but clear,
Tales of love and loss draw near.

Each heartbeat a rhythm, a timeless song,
A melody of souls that long,
To find solace in the endless night,
Where echoes fade into the light.

In the dance of time, they intertwine,
Echoes of a love divine,
Boundless, eternal, evermore,
In the echoes that forever soar.
